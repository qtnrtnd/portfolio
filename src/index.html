Versatility, creativity, adaptability.

I designed, developed, hosted this website and created its content. Just like I could do for you ! Wait, you just want a logo or a poster design ? That's great because I also know graphic design ! Yes, I am a « creative developer » as they say. I combine technique and art to deliver strong and impactful messages through my work. Otherwise, I have additional skills directly related to my hobbies: music production, sound design or sound mixing.

Many skills but only one goal.

What is the purpose of all this ? Providing the best possible experience for users. In a world where digital is evolving so quickly, we, communication professionals, must adapt and learn constantly in order to be able to make the most of the possibilities offered by new technologies and propose unique experiences to users. That's why I like experimenting with new tools such as computer-generated imagery for instance.

A professionalizing university education.

I am currently in BUT MMI at Rennes University in France. We are trained in all the disciplines encompassed by the digital world (graphic design, web development, audiovisual but also project management and marketing). Our school projects are directly linked to the professional world since we work with real clients (associations, companies). This allows us to deal with common problems and pushes us to collaborate through group work.
